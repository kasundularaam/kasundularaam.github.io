About me

associate ai engineer · battaramulla, sri lanka

I build AI features for real applications. The part I find most interesting is autonomy — how an agent decides, and how to make a system that reasons, retrieves, and acts without a human in every step. I try to stay tech-agnostic and work from first principles: understand the problem, find the real bottleneck, then build.

I earned my BSc (Hons) in Data Science from SLTC Research University, with early industrial exposure as an AI/Data Engineer Intern at Michelin Lanka. Today I work as an Associate AI Engineer at iLabs, where I've architected agentic assistants, knowledge-graph pipelines, and LLM observability stacks. Before all that — and still on the side — I spent years freelancing, shipping 50+ web, mobile and IoT apps.

what i'm into

  • Reinforcement learning — a clear way to frame an agent learning to achieve goals.
  • Agentic systems — orchestrators, sub-agents, tools, and making them reliable.
  • Knowledge graphs + RAG — giving models structure and memory, not just a context window.
  • LLM observability — being able to see what a model is actually doing.
